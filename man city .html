Manchester City is an English football club based in Manchester, in the northwest of England. The club was founded in 1880 under the name "St. Mark's" and changed its name to Manchester City in 1894. Manchester City is considered one of the top clubs in English and European football, having won numerous national and international titles throughout its long history.
