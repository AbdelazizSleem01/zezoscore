Napoli, also known as Napoli Football Club, is an Italian professional football club based in the city of Naples. The club was founded in 1926 and has since become one of the most successful clubs in Italian football. Napoli has won two Serie A titles, six Coppa Italia trophies, two Supercoppa Italiana titles, and one UEFA Cup. The club has also had success in European competitions, reaching the quarter-finals of the UEFA Champions League on several occasions. The club's home stadium is the Stadio San Paolo, which has a capacity of over 60,000 spectators. The team's colors are sky blue and white, and its nickname is "I Partenopei," which refers to the ancient Greek city that was located in the same area as modern-day Naples.
